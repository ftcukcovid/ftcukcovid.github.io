Privacy Policy

This website only collects informaiton about you in aggregate form for the purposes of monitoring and ensuring service. Google Analytics is used to collect the following informaiton:

Usage Information: Information about your use of this website. For example, information about specific actions perfomred-in other words, what you did, when and which object (for example, clicking a link).

Location Information: The approximate location of your device from your IP address is collected, your IP address remains hidden. This information is used to calculate how many people from different geographic regions visit the site.

Informaiton from Cookiess: A cookie is a string of informaiton that a website stores on a visitors computer, and that the visitors browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. This website uses cookies to provide the tracking described above.

Aggregated Information: Information that has been aggregated, so that the information could not reasonably be used to identify you. For instance, I may publish aggregate statistics about the use of this website.

Your Choices
